About

Hackathon

We're hosting the third installment of our Student Hackathon in the University of Sheffield, open to students across the country! We provide the WiFi, power, and food while teams of hackers create something new and exciting.

Venue

This year, we're moving into a new home to cope with our new capacity. 38 Mappin Street is a newly reopened building nearby the Diamond with a much bigger capacity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HackSheffield?

It's a Hackathon in the University of Sheffield, open to students around the world! We provide WiFi, power, and food while you and your team create something new.

What's a 'Hackathon'?

A Hackathon is a programming marathon, where teams of 2-4 put their ideas together to create something exciting in only 24 hours. It gives teams of hackers a chance to turn their ideas into reality.

Can I attend?

You missed it! But usually: to attend as a hacker, you need to be a university student or have left university in the past year. Apologies, but we could not accept under 18s at that time.

Travel reimbursements?

We were unable to offer any form of travel reimbursement for this event.

What about sleep?

If you wanted to sleep, we had some lecture theatres where you could get some Z's.

What did I need to bring?

Your ticket, Student ID and a laptop with its charger. That's about it!

How do I register?

You missed it! We released tickets in rounds. They became available on our EventBrite. RSVP'ing on our Facebook Event got you notifications!
