Frequently Asked Questions

What is HackSheffield?

It's a Hackathon in the University of Sheffield, open to students across the country! We provide WiFi, power, and food while you and your team create something new.

What's a 'Hackathon'?

A Hackathon is a programming marathon, where teams of 2-4 put their ideas together to create something exciting in only 24 hours. It gives teams of hackers a chance to turn their ideas into reality.

Can I attend?

To attend as a hacker, you need to be a university student or have left university since the 22nd October 2015. Apologies, but we cannot accept under 18s at this time.

Travel reimbursements?

At this time, we cannot promise any travel reimbursements since our budget has not been finalised.

What about sleep?

If you want to sleep (please do), we are providing quiet rooms where people can get some Z's.
