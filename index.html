FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

We're hosting a Hackathon in the University of Sheffield, but it's open to anyone in the country! The task is to create a solution in teams to a given problem.

What's a hackathon?

An event where you are given a problem to create a solution for within a set time!

I'm under 18. Can I come?

We would like to extend the offer to under 18s however we need to ensure there are safety measures in place before we can confirm under 18s can attend.

How do I register?

Registration isn't open yet, but you can register your interest to be notified when more information is available.

Will I be photographed?

There will be a photographer at the event, however please let us know if you do not wish to be photographed.
